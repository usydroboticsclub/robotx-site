USYD RowBot represents more than 20 undergraduate students studying mechatronics engineering at the University of Sydney, each of whom are eager to extend their knowledge in the field of robotics well beyond their coursework. To complement its engineering core, the team brings together a wide range of experience with many students adding to their mechatronics studies with arts, business, and science degrees.

We are also working closely with the Australian Centre for Field Robotics, who are mentoring us, and providing lab-space and equipment to aid our projects.
