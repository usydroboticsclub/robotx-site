RobotX 2020

RobotX is an international competition run by RoboNation focused on Maritime Automation. An automated Wam-V based system is designed to complete a series of high level challenges which require integrating computer vision, accurate navigation and a number of sensors.


We will be competing for the first time in the 3rd iteration of the competition in December 2018 in Hawaii.
